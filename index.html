How it works

A guided financing experience designed to be clear, structured, and straightforward.

1

Choose your equipment

Select the configuration that fits your needs. For example:

  • A compact carry setup (compact + red dot + mounting)
  • Or a more advanced configuration (SBR + suppressor + red dot)

Your merchant confirms eligibility within program guidelines.

2

Complete required paperwork

Where required, complete the necessary forms and pass any applicable background checks (e.g., NICS). Financing proceeds only after eligibility requirements are satisfied.

3

Apply

Enter your basic information and receive an instant, policy-driven outcome.

4

Review your offer

Your offer clearly outlines:

  • Financing term
  • Estimated monthly payment
  • Defined purchase option (residual)
  • Early buyout schedule (when eligible)

Your cost basis is composed of: Ticket + Acquisition Fee + Sales Tax + Merchant Processing.

5

Sign & fulfill

Agreements are delivered through e-signature and tied to item fulfillment. Sign your documents, complete your first payment, and take home your equipment.

6

Choose your path at end of term

At maturity, you may return, renew, or purchase at the defined residual amount. Eligible accounts may access a transparent early buyout schedule so you’re never left guessing.

Transparent pricing inputs and definitions (no “mystery fees”). Predictable schedule with clear end-of-term options.
