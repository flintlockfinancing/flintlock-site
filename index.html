Policy-driven consumer leasing, built for auditability

Flintlock Financing is a synthetic demonstration environment used to illustrate credit policy controls, pricing governance, disclosures, and contract artifact generation.

What this demo shows

  • Policy controls that gate approvals (credit box, caps, term rules).
  • Pricing controls using simple storyboard math (money factor + residual).
  • Disclosures & artifacts (Reg M-style box + contract preview + exhibits).
  • Exports (CSV/JSON schedules for integration demos).
Important: This website is for demos and testing only. No identity verification, credit pulls, funding, or payments occur here.
