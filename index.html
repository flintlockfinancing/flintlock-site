How it works

A straightforward, transparent process — designed to be fast for customers, clean for merchants, and disciplined for capital partners.

1

Choose your equipment

Select what you want to purchase — for example a compact carry option, a red dot, and mounting (if applicable), or a more advanced setup such as an SBR with a suppressor and optic.

2

Complete required paperwork

If your item requires it, complete the necessary paperwork and checks (including a background check / NICS). Eligibility checks happen before the financing flow begins.

3

Apply

Enter your basic information and receive an instant (illustrative) outcome based on a defined, no-exceptions credit policy and verification gates.

4

Review your offer

See your term, payment estimate, and buyout options clearly — including what goes into your cost basis: ticket + acquisition fee + tax + merchant processing (all capitalized).

5

Sign & fulfill

In production, agreements are delivered through e-signature and tied to item delivery/fulfillment. Sign your documents, make your first payment, and take home your new item.

6

Choose your path at end of term

Return, renew, or purchase at a defined option price (residual). Don’t feel trapped — if eligible, you’ll have an early monthly buyout schedule for transparency.

Built for disciplined verification

Faster than traditional underwriting — and less exposed to ad-hoc exceptions. Flintlock is designed around clear policy gates and a verification workflow that stays consistent. Manual review is reserved for fraud/identity alerts (e.g., insufficient information), not credit-policy exceptions.
