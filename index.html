How it works

A straightforward, controlled process from point of sale to structured lease offer.

1. Merchant submits transaction

Approved merchants initiate a lease request at point of sale and confirm required eligibility conditions prior to application.

2. Customer completes application

The applicant provides identity and financial information through a structured workflow governed by predefined underwriting criteria.

3. Lease offer presented

If approved, a consumer lease offer is generated with structured disclosures, including payment schedule, total of payments, and purchase option terms.

No hidden fees. No ambiguous structures. No discretionary guesswork.
